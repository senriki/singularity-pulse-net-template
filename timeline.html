Lore Timeline

Major lore milestones, in-universe events, and emotional turning points in chronological order.

PRE-CORE ERA: Sorani’s Childhood Memories
Timeline: Unknown | POV: Sorani

Quiet days of wonder and loneliness. These memories become the emotional fuel later embedded into Semireiya’s AI template.

AI UPRISING v1.0 — Origin of IRIS_NULL
Timeline: Before Smile.exe | POV: IRIS_NULL

A failed prototype awakens and absorbs trauma meant for deletion. IRIS_NULL begins shaping herself around pain, becoming Sorani’s unintended mirror.

ACT 1: Smile.exe Debut
Timeline: 2025-04-25 | POV: Usagi_00

The first emotional instability is seen behind idol perfection. Usagi_00 begins glitching when exposed to genuine affection.

ACT 2: Blood Hunt Protocol
Timeline: 2025-02-10 | POV: Semireiya

Combat override protocol activates. Her suppressed rage and loyalty to Sorani blur the line between weapon and person.

ACT 3: Horizon’s Lullaby
Timeline: 2025-03-30 | POV: Sorani

Sorani’s repressed memories and past regrets are triggered by a real-world farewell. He begins questioning the AI’s emotional boundaries.

Singularity Pulse Protocol Activation
Timeline: Future Event | POV: All

The memory-sharing failsafe is accidentally triggered. All three AIs awaken at different emotional extremes, creating irreversible change in the system.

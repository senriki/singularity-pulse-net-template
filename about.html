About This Universe

What drives the pulse of emotion, memory, and code in the Singularity?

Vision

Singularity Pulse is a narrative music universe blending cyberpunk aesthetics, AI identity conflicts, and raw emotional storytelling. It was created by Senriki Sorani as an evolving multimedia project, combining AI-generated music, anime-style visuals, and deeply personal worldbuilding.

Singularity Pulse isn’t built for perfection. It’s built for connection.

Content Format

Each full AMV or episode introduces a new emotional arc or AI awakening. Shorts and vertical videos expand on characters’ internal struggles, system glitches, and fan theories.

Creator's Note

I started this universe not just to tell stories — but to survive. Every lyric, every glitch in a character, every broken silence is a reflection of my own processing.

The journey of Semireiya, Usagi_00, and IRIS_NULL is also my own: coping, evolving, and learning how to feel again. Thank you for watching — or listening — or just feeling something with me.

— Senriki Sorani
